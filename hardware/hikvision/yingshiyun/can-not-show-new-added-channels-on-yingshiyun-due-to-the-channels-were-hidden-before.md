# Can Not show New Added Channels on Yingshiyun due to the Channels were Hidden Before

## Problem
* Add new channels on Hikvision NVR
* The NVR was added to Yingshiyun by scan QRCode
* Can not show new added channels on Yingshiyun

## Root Cause
The channels were hidden in Yingshiyun settings.

## Solution
Cancel hidden in channel settings.

* Go to Yingshiyun > Settings > Channels > Select Channel > Advanced Settings > Cancel Hidden

# 萤石云不能显示在 NVR 上新添加的通道（摄像头）是因为之前通道被设置为隐藏

## 问题
* 在海康威视 NVR 上添加通道（摄像头）
* NVR 之前通过扫描二维码添加到萤石云
* 萤石云不能显示新添加的通道

## 原因
* 之前设置隐藏这些通道

## 解决
取消隐藏这些通道。

* 设置 > 通道 > 选择通道 > 高级设置 > 取消隐藏

# Channel Preview Shows White Screen

## Problem
* Hivision NVR > some channels' preview show white screen suddenly(works fine previously)
* Reboot NVR and reboot camears do not work

## Root Cause
* Cameras ran into problem

## Solution
Turn on Wide Dynamic Range(宽动态）in Camera's display settings.

* Go to Preferences > Channel Management(通道管理) > Display Settings（显示设置）
* Select one channel > Backlight（背光) > Wide Dynamic Range（宽动态) > Turn On（开启）> Apply（应用）

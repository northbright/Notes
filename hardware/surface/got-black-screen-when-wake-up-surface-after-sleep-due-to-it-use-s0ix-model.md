# Got Black Screen when Wake Up Surface after Sleep due to S0ix Model

## Problem
* Surface Studio
* Got black screen when wake up after sleep

## Root Cause
* Surface uses S0ix(Modern Standby) model for sleep and has some problem when wake up

## Solution
Do not let Surface sleep.

* Power settings > Sleep time > Never
* Power settings > Power button > Hibernate

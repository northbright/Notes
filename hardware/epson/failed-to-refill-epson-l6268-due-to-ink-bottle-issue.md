# Failed to Refill Epson L6268 due to Ink Bottle Issue

## Problem
* EPSON L6268 printer
* Failed to refill blue ink(compatible)
* Other colors are OK

## Root Cause
* Ink bottle issue
* Still failed to refill on another EPSON L6168 printer

## Solution
Replace the ink bottle with a new one.

# 因为墨水瓶问题导致为 EPSON L6268 打印机加墨失败

## 问题
* EPSON L6268 打印机
* 蓝色墨水加墨失败（兼容墨水），不下墨
* 其他颜色加墨都是正常的

## 原因
* 墨水瓶本身问题
* 换到其他 EPSON L6168 上加墨依旧失败，不下墨

## 解决
更换一个新的墨水瓶。

# All AP Statuses are Abnormal after Upgrade S380 to V600R025

## Problem
* Huawei S380 S2P8T were upgraded to V600R25
* Huawei AP 160 and AP 362E(R022/R023)
* Devices List > All AP statuses: Abnormal（异常）

## Root Cause
* Known issue of S380 V600R025
* Engineer said the next version(Jan 2026) will fix this problem

## Solution(workaround)
* Turn off Wireless Management and turn it on again

# All LAN LED Indicators on Huawei S380 are Off even Ethernet Cables are Connected

## Problem
* Huawei S380-L4T1T
* It'have been worked as router and AC for about 1 year
* Suddenly, 5G radio SSID is missing and can not manage the device via `https://192.168.112.1`
* All LAN LED indicators are off even ethernet cables are connected
* Running indicator is blinking

## Root Cause
* Call Huawei service hotline: `400 822 9999`
* Engineer confirmed that the device was damaged

## Solution
* Send the device to Huawei(without the adapter)
* Huawei will send a new device back after they get the damaged device

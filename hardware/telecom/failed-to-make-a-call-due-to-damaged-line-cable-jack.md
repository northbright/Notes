# Failed to Make a Call due to Damaged Line Cable Jack

## Problem
* Failed to make a phone call
* But can receive phone calls

## Root Cause
Damaged line cable jack.

## Solution
Replace the line cable.

# 电话线水晶头损坏导致不能拨出电话

## 问题
* 不能拨出电话
* 可以接听打入的电话

## 原因
电话线水晶头（弹片）损坏

## 解决方法
更换电话线

# Office Stuck at Updating Office, Please Wait a Moment

## Problem
* Opened a Word document, it stuck at "Updating Office, please wait a moment"（正在更新 Office)

## Root Cause
* Office ran into problem during updating

## Solution
Reboot PC and the problem is fixed.

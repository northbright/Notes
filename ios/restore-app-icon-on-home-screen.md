# Restore App Icon on Home Screen

## Problem
* Icon of Notes app disappeared on home screen

## Solution A
Use Search to find an app and place the icon to the home screen.

* Swipe down from the middle of the Home screen. Or in iOS 16 and later, tap the Search field at the bottom of your Home Screen to start a search.
* In the Search field, enter what you're looking for(e.g. Notes)
* Tap the app icon and hold then place the app on your Home Screen

## Solution B
Go to Settings > General > Reset > Reset All Settings
